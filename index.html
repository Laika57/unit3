The Soviet Union

Information

The USSR(United Soviet Socialist Republics) was created via the Bolshevik Revolution, the Bolsheviks(Communist) party is based on Marxist socialism. The USSR is usually attributed with being the first "communist" country. Even though they were not actually communist, they were the step before known as socialism. Socialism is basically just a weaker and watered down version of communism. Either way, both ideologies focus on the workers, or the proletariat. The workers try hard to take down the bourgeoisie. Communism is practically the opposite of capitalism. The leader of the revolution was Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov. He was also known as Vladimir Lenin. He was the first leader of the Soviet Union.

Their Role in World War 2

The Soviets role in the war was mostly being defeated and pushed back all the way to their capital(Moscow) then coming back in the cruel Russian winter and destroying the Germans. The Soviets surrounded Berlin and took the city. One quote by Stalin upon hearing of Hitler's suicide, was "So the bastard's dead? Too bad we didn't capture him alive!" Lots of people say that the only reason that the Germans fell to the Soviets was because of the Russian Winter. But it was mix of tactical mistakes and missing oil. Also at the beginning of the war, the Soviets were majorly unprepared for the challenge of holding back the German blitzkrieg(Lighting War) A blitzkrieg is where the Germans very quickly rushed across the border using their fastest soldiers. They were mostly made out of panzers. But after a while and, after Stalin came out of his deep depression and prolonged near anxiety attack, they began putting up a defensive.

The Collapse

Unfortunately or some and fortunately for others, the Soviet Union like all countries will, collapsed. The reason it collapsed was because the current actual president, Mikhail Gorbachev, was essentially useless and sped up the collapse with his dismal reforms. Also they had started to lose control on Eastern Europe. They were making less and less money in oil and gas. Also their civilians had next to no food. There was a massive famine because they tried to push for industrialization.Also, Stalin's collectivist farm idea was a terrible one. Ironically, money was the thing that brought the down the Socialist Union that aspired to become truly communist.
